Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Skills ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by the Skills application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data (e.g., screens visited, features used) via PostHog to improve the app.
  • Local Data: Your skills, timers, streaks, and session history are stored locally on your device using an SQLite database (Drift).
  • Purchase Information: If you make an in-app purchase, Apple and RevenueCat securely process the transaction. We do not have access to your payment details.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the application.
  • To analyze usage trends and track crash reports so we can fix bugs.
  • To manage your subscription status for Premium access.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • PostHog (Analytics)
  • RevenueCat (Subscription Management)
  • Supabase (Backend infrastructure)

4. DATA RETENTION & DELETION

Because your skill data and history are stored locally, you can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

5. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. CONTACT US
